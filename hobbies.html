My Musical Interests

Instrument Background and Repertoire
Flamenco : I started playing Mandoin when I was 5 years old under one of the best Mandolin players of India, Saroj Barua Mandolin clip. After moving to USA, I trained under the Mary Akerman Recuerdos de al Alhambra, one of the best classical guitarists of the country and a GFA Winnder. After attending a concert by the Great Paco De Lucia in a concert, I discovered my true calling of the flamenco guitar tradition. The harmonical beauty, rhythmic intricacies and feel somehow attracted me to use my backgrounds of everything I learned in Mandolina and Classical Guitar. Learning flamenco in USA is not easy as there are only a handful of truly great teachers and I was fortunate to find Ricardo and his superlative playing at the Maestro level and incredible teaching gave me the confidence that it is possible to learn this art
Drums : I love rhythm. In particular, I am trying to learn to play the drum set with the intention of building a repertoire to accompany Reggae and solo linear drumming grooves
