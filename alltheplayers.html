Applied formal methods

Pragmatic formal specification, verification and synthesis of distributed concurrent systems and cyber physical systems.

  • Distributed systems : Use of theorem proving and model checking techniques to find either certificates for certain critical properties of designed systems
    • Coq, TLA+ and Alloy
  • Cyber physical systems : Dynamics described by ODEs and computation descibed by automatons; properties are defined by temporal logic
    • Keyamera, Murphi
